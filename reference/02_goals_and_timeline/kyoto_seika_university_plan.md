# Kyoto Seika University Plan

## Target Institution
**Kyoto Seika University (京都精华大学)**

## Why Kyoto Seika?

### Primary Strengths
- First university in Japan to establish a manga department (漫画学部)
- "Holy land" of Japanese manga education
- Strong in character design (角色设计) and story manga (故事漫画)
- Free, even "wild" campus atmosphere that attracts independent creators
- Located in Kyoto - slower pace than Tokyo, rich cultural heritage

### Why It's Ideal for QolarBear
1. **VN Development Alignment**: Kyoto Seika's character design and story manga programs are perfectly tailored for visual novel development
2. **Storytelling Focus**: Unlike pure art schools, Kyoto Seika emphasizes "driving narrative through visuals" - exactly what VNs require
3. **Indie Studio DNA**: The campus attracts students interested in independent development and publishing - potential future collaborators
4. **Cultural Fit**: Kyoto's atmosphere suits QolarBear's preference for deep thinking and life observation

## Target Department
**Manga Department (漫画学部)** with focus on:
- Character Design (角色设计)
- Story Manga (故事漫画)

## Competition Reality
- **High Competition**: Even as a private university, the manga department has extremely high competition rates
- **International Student Challenge**: For international students, Japanese N2 is the minimum, N1 is the safe standard
- **Portfolio Weight**: Kyoto Seika professors care more about "what kind of person you are" than technical mastery

## Alternative Universities Considered

| University | Strengths | Relevance to QolarBear |
|------------|-----------|------------------------|
| **Tama Art University (多摩美术大学)** | Graphic design,统筹感, commercial aesthetics | Best for world-class visual packaging of VNs |
| **Tokyo Polytechnic University (东京工艺大学)** | Games, animation, new media | Strong game department for systematic game development learning |
| **Tokyo University of the Arts (东京艺术大学)** | Fine arts, traditional | Too traditional; not aligned with VN/commercial art goals |

## Strategic Decision
**Primary Target**: Kyoto Seika University (漫画学部/角色设计)
**Backup**: Tama Art University or Tokyo Polytechnic University

## Mentor's Assessment
> "Visual novel is a track that heavily depends on personal depth. Excellent VNs (like Steins;Gate, Mahoutsukai no Yoru) become classics not because of无敌画工, but because of the author's cognition, experience, and that 'interest.' Your current 'going with the flow' and reading are accumulating energy for future scripts. But remember: scripts without technical support are just web novels; paintings without narrative ability are just materials."
