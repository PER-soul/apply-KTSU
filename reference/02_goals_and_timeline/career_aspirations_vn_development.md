# Career Aspirations: Visual Novel Development

## Ultimate Dream
**Found an indie studio for visual novel (VN) game development**

## Role Aspirations
- **Art Director**: Responsible for visual aesthetics and character design
- **Scriptwriter**: Responsible for story, narrative structure, and dialogue

## Why Visual Novels?

### Personal Connection
QolarBear is also writing novels, and VNs represent the perfect fusion of his two main creative drives:
1. **Visual Art**: Creating beauty through images
2. **Storytelling**: Crafting narratives and experiences

### The VN Advantage
Visual novels are "extremely dependent on personal depth" - they require:
- Deep cognitive foundation
- Rich life experiences
- That unique "interest" (趣味) that cannot be faked

> "Excellent VNs (like Steins;Gate, Mahoutsukai no Yoru) become classics not because of无敌画工, but because of the author's cognition, experience, and that 'interest.'"

## Core Design Philosophy

### Player Agency
Inspired by Bilibili UP主 "童子军大王" (Boy Scout King):
> **"Give the power of choice to players; let their choices construct their unique story."**

This means:
- Meaningful branching narratives
- Player decisions shape the story
- Multiple valid paths and endings

### AI Integration Concept
QolarBear has conceived an ambitious idea:
> **"Can I introduce AI into VNs to let them change character dialogue and story direction in real-time based on player choices - even tiny differences - without deviating from the outline and character settings? Like a real-time story calculator."**

**Technical Feasibility**: 
- Current LLMs (Large Language Models) with Agent architecture can potentially achieve this
- Character.ai or locally deployed Llama models as foundation
- Variables (x) = player choices; AI maps within character constraints [A,B]

**Current Status**: Conceptual stage; feasibility unclear

## Long-Term Vision

### The "Interest" Filter
QolarBear's VN projects will embody his core philosophy:
- Reject market-driven mediocrity
- Create works that are genuinely "interesting"
- Build a studio culture that values creativity over productivity

### AI Era Positioning
QolarBear believes AI will:
- Flatten technical skill differences in art
- Shift competition to "aesthetic decision-making" and "imagination"
- Filter between "those who only practice technique" vs "those with technique AND interest"

> "AI fills the demand for mediocre aesthetics. You must use your 'interest' to create demand that didn't previously exist."
