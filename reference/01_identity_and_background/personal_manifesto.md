## Introduction

Call me QolarBear - "South Pole Bear" in Chinese. I think it's a fairly memorable name.

## Main Quest: Art and Japanese

I view painting as my main quest. My biggest goal right now is learning to paint and studying Japanese, giving everything I have to get into a Japanese art university (this is extremely difficult for me). I love drawing, even though my current work is terrible, but things will get better.

## Side Quests

My side quests are:
- Enhancing cognition
- Experiencing stories
- Learning instruments I love and slowly reaching a high level

These aren't as urgent as improving my painting skills. They can be completed slowly over many years of gradual accumulation, or they may be endless like painting.

## On Talent and Creation

I firmly believe that things requiring heartfelt experience like painting, to reach the extreme, the pinnacle - high-level drawing ability is just the foundation of foundations. The steps to the altar need to be built with cognition and experience. Music is the same.

So I hate but believe in so-called "talent." It definitely exists and definitely helps a lot, but it cannot bring the unique cognition and experience that belongs only to oneself. You can see painting "geniuses" everywhere online, but how many have created truly expressive, living works that express themselves?

## The Concept of "Interest" (趣味)

I have some strange abstract theories (that I can't even articulate in technical language), the most used being what I call "interest":

> **"Interest" is the key to opening the door of artistic creation, the cornerstone of all "living" artistic creation.**

I appreciate people with interest, regardless of their identity, status, or personality (this probably doesn't have good or bad), achievements - as long as they have "interest," they are my friends, true "living people" in my eyes.

## On AI and Art

My painting dream has been attacked by adults around me using AI many times. I don't want to repeat or evaluate the content - why bother analyzing the taste of shit after eating it?

I've discussed with Ceaser many times what AI's development will do to the painting industry. Our conclusion: nobody knows what will happen, let's see how it goes.

I believe colors and lines full of emotion will never be replaced by cold 0s and 1s. But who knows how AI will develop in the future, or how people will view it? Probably (very likely) AI will also have emotions. Then where will I go as a human artist? Who knows? Let's see how it goes.

**Sisyphus is happy.**

## Why Painting?

I've thought about why I'm so obsessed with painting. The answer I later came to is probably because my heart is always full of the impulse to create beauty. So painting is actually just one means for me. I also love photography, writing, and music - painting is just my favorite way.

## Closing

In short, this is QolarBear, forever full of the impulse to experience the world and stories.

**Lifelong thinking, infinite creation.**

**"Interest" illuminates the world.**
