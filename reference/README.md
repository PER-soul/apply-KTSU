## Key Concepts

### "Interest" (趣味)
The cornerstone of QolarBear's philosophy - the quality of being alive, creative, and authentically engaged. The opposite of vulgarity (庸俗).

### Anti-Fragile Learning
Learning strategy inspired by Nassim Taleb:
- Embrace volatility
- Via Negativa (define by removing bad)
- Barbell Strategy (balance extremes)
- Optionality (keep multiple paths)

### Encoding Summary (编码总结)
Converting learning into named, structured knowledge:
> "Only knowledge that can be named is prey you've captured."

### Self-Sovereignty
> "I am my only motivation."

Rejection of external motivation; internal drive as the only sustainable force.
