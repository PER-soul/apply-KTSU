## Fermentation Methodology

### The Process
1. **Catch**: Recognize valuable thought/question
2. **Ferment**: Don't force immediate resolution
3. **Wait**: Trust subconscious processing
4. **Capture**: Document when insight emerges
5. **Integrate**: Connect to existing knowledge

### Distinguishing Fermentation from Procrastination
**Fermentation**:
- Active subconscious processing
- Eventually produces insight
- Accompanied by continued input/practice

**Procrastination**: Passive avoidance
- No eventual output
- Used to逃避 difficult work

### Risk Factors
- Without "input" (practice/study), fermentation becomes "cognitive escape"
- Topics can be "swallowed" (forgotten) if not periodically revisited
- Over-fermentation without action becomes高级拖延
